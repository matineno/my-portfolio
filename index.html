Get to know me

Since my high school years, I've been deeply passionate about the latest technological developments. Following high school, I interned for a year at a media studio in Nigeria, where I honed my skills in video and movie editing using Adobe Premiere Pro and Adobe After Effects. This experience significantly heightened my interest in the creative and development realms.

In 2016, I pursued a two-year diploma in computer engineering, which I completed in 2018. Following this, I enrolled in the IBM Digital Nation for Africa program, aimed at educating African students about emerging technologies. During this program, I developed a keen interest in programming, particularly with Python, and gained proficiency in the basics of web development.

After completing my training, I joined Gleaming Stars Schools, a British-based institution, as an IT consultant and teacher. There, I furthered my workplace skills and contributed to teaching children programming using Scratch Desktop. I remained with the school until 2020 when I had to cease working due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

During this period, my interest in design flourished, and I began working as a freelance graphics designer. I collaborated with small companies, enhancing my skills while also undertaking online web development courses on platforms like Udemy and Coursera to broaden my programming expertise.

Determined to advance my skills and transition my hobbies into a profession, I decided to pursue studies in Canada. I applied to the Software Development program at the Manitoba Institute of Trades and Technology and have since been pursuing my career as a web developer in Canada.
